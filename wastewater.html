Web App Showing Toronto's Wastewater Management

Follow Your Flush is an interactive 3D web app that traces where wastewater travels after a Toronto flush. Click anywhere in the city and it finds the treatment plant serving that spot, animates a camera flythrough along the route, and follows the outfall out to the lake. It is a science-communication tool that came about after conversations with friends and family, where I noticed how little awareness there was of the city's sewage system, a gap that in turn severs people's connection to nature. The map received an Honorable Mention in the David Woodward Student Digital Map Award at the 2025 CartoGIS Map Design Competition.
